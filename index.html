About Jose

After graduating from college with a Bachelor's and Master's in Music, I worked in a variety of jobs, from elementary school technology instructor to instructional designer for UCLA.

I have a natural love and passion for teaching and inspiring young people to believe in themselves, instilling in them that, with hard work and determination, anything is possible.

As a student at Thinkful, I look forward to expanding on my knowledge of UX/UI as well as become proficient in web and software development.

Please reach out if you're looking for someone who's creative and not afraid to take on new challenges. I look forward to working with a diverse team of developers-- from different backgrounds and experience levels-- on software that will change the world.
